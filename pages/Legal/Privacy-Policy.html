Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

1. Introduction

At MagicBG, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect basic information such as your email address, uploaded image files, and usage statistics to improve the quality of our background removal service.

3. Third-Party API Usage

MagicBG uses the following third-party services for background removal:

  • Remove.bg API: Provides fast and high-quality background removal using advanced AI.
  • Pixian.ai API: Used for lightweight background processing, especially for low-bandwidth users.
  • MediaPipe: Google's open-source framework used for on-device image segmentation and background removal.

By uploading an image, you consent to having it processed through one or more of these services depending on optimization and performance.

4. Image Handling & Deletion

Uploaded images are processed in real-time and deleted from our servers shortly after processing. We do not store your files permanently unless explicitly requested for future use.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience and gather anonymized analytics. This includes IP address, browser type, and page interactions.

6. Data Sharing & Security

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We use encryption and secure channels to protect your data and communicate with third-party APIs securely.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, update, or delete your personal information. You may also withdraw consent at any time by contacting our team via the contact page.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review it periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact page.
