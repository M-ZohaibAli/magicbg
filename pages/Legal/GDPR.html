GDPR Compliance

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

1. Introduction

At MagicBG, we take your privacy seriously and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679. This page outlines your rights and how we handle your personal data.

2. What Data We Collect

When using MagicBG, we may collect the following information:

  • Email address (for contact or subscription forms)
  • Uploaded image metadata (only during active use)
  • Browser and device type (for analytics and optimization)
  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the background removal service
  • Improve our website’s performance and user experience
  • Communicate with users (e.g., support, feature updates)
  • Analyze traffic and prevent misuse of services

4. Third-Party APIs and Data Transfer

We integrate background removal APIs such as Remove.bg, Pixian.ai, and MediaPipe. Your uploaded images may be temporarily transferred to these services for processing. We ensure all vendors comply with privacy standards and delete image data after processing.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Object to or restrict data processing

6. Data Storage and Security

We store minimal user data and use Firebase for contact form submissions. All data transfers are encrypted via HTTPS. We do not permanently store images uploaded for background removal.

7. Data Retention

Uploaded images are processed in real time and discarded immediately afterward. Contact form entries may be stored securely in Firebase until they are addressed and deleted periodically.

8. Cookie Usage

Cookies are used to personalize your experience and analyze traffic. For more information, read our Cookie Policy.

9. Contact for GDPR Requests

If you wish to exercise your rights or have GDPR-related questions, please use our contact form or email us directly.
