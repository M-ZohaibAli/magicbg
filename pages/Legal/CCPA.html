CCPA Compliance

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

1. Overview

This page explains how MagicBG complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), which provides California residents with rights regarding their personal data.

2. Personal Information We Collect

  • Email address (if provided in contact form)
  • Uploaded image metadata (temporary use)
  • IP address and browser information
  • Device data for performance optimization

3. How We Use This Information

  • To process and remove image backgrounds
  • To analyze site performance and improve UX
  • To respond to inquiries or support requests

4. Your Rights Under CCPA

As a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we collect and why
  • Access your personal data upon request
  • Request deletion of your data (with exceptions)
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

5. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with third-party APIs (Remove.bg, Pixian.ai, MediaPipe) solely for the purpose of processing your uploaded images securely.

6. Exercising Your Rights

You can request access, deletion, or more information about your data by using our Contact Page.

7. Authorized Agents

You may authorize another person or entity to act on your behalf by providing a signed, written authorization or legal power of attorney document.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this CCPA page from time to time. The latest version will always be available here and marked with the updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about your CCPA rights, contact us via our Contact Form.
