Cookie Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help enhance user experience by remembering your preferences and usage behavior.

2. How We Use Cookies

MagicBG uses cookies to:

  • Improve website functionality and performance.
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior via analytics tools.
  • Remember your settings and previous actions for a smoother experience.
  • Support personalized content or language preferences.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality like login and navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies: Track page usage anonymously to improve our service (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember user settings (like dark/light mode or language preference).
  • Third-party Cookies: Set by tools we use (e.g., Remove.bg, Pixian.ai API) for optimization and image processing.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate cookies from trusted third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: To collect anonymized usage data.
  • Remove.bg / Pixian.ai: May place cookies for API optimization.
  • Firebase: Used for contact submissions and may store user session data.

5. Managing Your Cookies

You can control or delete cookies at any time by:

  • Adjusting browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Using browser extensions or privacy plugins to manage cookie behavior.
  • Opting out of Google Analytics via the official opt-out tool.

6. Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy unless you have disabled them in your browser.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. All updates will be posted on this page with the revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please reach out through our contact page.
