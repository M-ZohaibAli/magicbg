Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MagicBG

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will take effect immediately, and you'll receive a prorated credit or charge based on your billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or students?

Yes, we offer special discounts for non-profit organizations and students. Please contact our sales team with proof of your status to learn more about our discounted plans.

What happens if I exceed my monthly background removal limit?

If you exceed your monthly limit on the Pro plan, you'll be charged a small fee for each additional background removal. On the Free plan, you'll need to wait until your credits reset at the start of the next month.

Is there a minimum contract term?

No, all our plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. You can cancel at any time with no cancellation fees.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover. For Enterprise plans, we also accept bank transfers and ACH payments.
